About DC‑714

DC‑714 is a community‑run local DEFCON group in Orange County, CA. We meet monthly for lightning talks, demos, workshops, and casual hallwaycon. All skill levels welcome.

  • When: 2nd Wednesday (except Jan & Aug), 6–8 PM
  • Where: Coastline Garden Grove Center — 12901 S. Euclid St., Garden Grove, CA — Room 251 “Storm Center”
  • Why: Learn, share, and build the local hacker community
  • Cost: Free. Zero budget, zero excuses.

This site is static (no backend, no trackers, no fees) and works on GitHub Pages or any basic web host.
